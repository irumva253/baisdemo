A Note by Our Principal, OTIM JOHN BOSCO

At Bright Angels international School, all learners matter! It is with pleasure that I extend a warm welcome to everyone.

Bright Angels International school offers Daycare services, Nursery, Primary, and Lower Secondary Cambridge and French Programs in collaboration with the Rwandan French Embassy and the British Council Rwanda.

We pride ourselves in having learners coming in a whole embracing vision to access International Programs at an affordable cost in both French and English.

We receive learners from Rwanda, the East African community at large, and many other corners of Africa and around the world. The diversity of our students is one of our greatest strengths.

Warm welcome,
Otim
