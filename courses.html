ACADEMICS DESCRIPTION

Daycare and Tiny Tots

Nos enseignants attentionnés établissent un rythme quotidien pour les tout-petits qui comprend un temps d'apprentissage en groupe, un temps de concentration scolaire individuel, une heure du conte, des jeux en plein air, des repas et des collations, des siestes et, plus particulièrement, beaucoup de temps de jeu (car nous savons que les tout-petits apprennent plus quand ils jouent avec des professeurs attentifs à proximité). Un grand terrain vert et des bacs à sable bien équipés permettent à nos petits anges de se déplacer librement et de jouer sous les soins professionnels de leurs enseignants.

Learning Programs in Nursery School

At Bright Angels International School, the Nursery School Programs are offered in Full French immersion from Daycare to Nursery 2. From Nursery 3, the child starts to familiarize with the Cambridge Primary Program which starts from 5 years old. Therefore, our learning time from that age enables the child to spend half of their day with an English Speaking teacher who guides them through educational activities in connection with English, Math, and Science after which half of the day is spent with a French Teacher who conducts activities related to the French Language, Themes' exploration, Life skills, Sensorial education, …. The development of French and English Languages ​​remains at the ''Heart'' of our learning programs in Nursery school.

Cambridge Primary Program

Cambridge Primary provides learners with a flexible framework which we use to tailor the curriculum to their needs. It provides assessment tools to help the school identify what children are learning, monitor their progress, and report to parents.
Cambridge Primary is excellent preparation for secondary school and for progression to other educational systems.

Enrichment Program

There is an organized support program at Bright Angels that aims to prepare learners to Perform Well during their CheckPoint exam organized by Cambridge Assessment International Education. We believe that extra practice and enrichment are from teacher success. Bright Angel's learners of Grades 5 and 6 stays behind in the afternoons on Wednesdays (Half Days for all other students) and Fridays (Clubs for all other students in the afternoons)
